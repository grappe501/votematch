VoteMatch

Spreadsheet-to-voter-file matching and review tools for petition signature tracking.

What it does

VoteMatch helps teams align petition signer rows with voter-file records and track counts for initiative work. It uses match confidence (technical match strength), not statements of legal validity. Rows may be marked review required when more information is needed—not as a legal conclusion.

At a high level

  • Spreadsheet matching against voter records
  • Confidence scoring
  • Review queue workflow
  • Ward and county summaries where your data supports it
  • Local and private handling of sensitive data

Actual matching, imports, and reports run locally (or in a secured environment you control)—not on this public page. This site is informational only: no uploads, no database, no voter data stored here.

Disclaimer

VoteMatch helps organize and review voter-file matches. It does not determine legal sufficiency or eligibility by itself.
